Fight Night
The pokemon are meeting tonight to find out the best brawler composition.
Help them by choosing two teams of 6 pokemon and find out who is the last one standing!
Each player will have a deck formed by the pokemon they choose, up to 6

Every turn a random pokemon will be drawn out from each deck and the strongest pokemon will be the winner.

The winner is decided based on its stats and each pokemon type weaknesses and strenghts

Roughly... (attack - deffense) * multiplier

The pokemon don't heal during the brawl
